# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on facts and solutions.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or doxxing.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, or access that violate this policy.
